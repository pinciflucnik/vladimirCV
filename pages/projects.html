"Orders tracker" was made with the sole purpose of facilitating the day to day delivery tracking of parts ordered through outside channels. It's made as a single page application with the use of the Bootstrap library, using back4app.com backend with pages as a router. It has a dynamic navigation with register (currently disabled), login, logout, orders list, completed orders list, new order items.
